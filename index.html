About KHAT

A company committed to innovation and ethical pet care.

Who We Are

KHAT is an innovation-driven technology company dedicated to improving pet safety and promoting responsible breeding. Our team is composed of developers, designers, and forward-thinkers who saw the rising problems caused by unsafe, unverified, and unregulated breeding practices.
With compassion and modern technology at our core, KHAT was created to give pet owners and breeders a safe, guided, and trustworthy platform that protects animals and supports ethical decision-making.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make pet breeding safe, transparent, and scientifically informed. We aim to reduce breeding risks, prevent unhealthy pairings, and promote responsible practices through verification, education, and intelligent digital tools.
KHAT exists to ensure that every breeding decision is made responsibly for the wellbeing of pets and the people who care for them.

Our Approach

We combine advanced technology with a strong commitment to animal welfare. Our approach ensures accuracy, safety, and trust at every stage of the breeding process through:

Strict verification systems

All users, breeders, shooters, and pets undergo thorough ID and health verification to ensure authenticity and safety.

AI-powered matching and risk detection

Advanced algorithms analyze breed traits, health history, and compatibility factors to recommend ideal matches and alert users to potential risks.

Ethical Standards

Transparency, accountability, and compassion guide every process, putting pet welfare above all.

Who Can Use PAWLINK

Designed for everyone in the pet breeding ecosystem

Pet Owners

Find safe, compatible breeding partners for your pets through AI-powered matching, verified health records, and clear, informed decision making.

Licensed Breeders

Professional Shooters

A Complete Ecosystem for Responsible Breeding

Whether you're a pet owner searching for the ideal match, a breeder managing multiple pairings, or a professional handler offering breeding support services, PAWLINK brings everyone together in a safe, verified, and ethical environment.

Introducing PAWLINK

Our flagship product - An AI-powered platform revolutionizing responsible pet breeding

1

User & Breeder Management

Secure account registration with government ID and selfie verification. The system differentiates regular pet owners from licensed breeders, ensuring proper credential validation and maintaining a trusted environment.

2

Pet Profiles & Verification

Create detailed pet profiles with unique Pet IDs, complete health information, and uploaded documents. Vetissued certificates such as Health, Vaccination, DNA/Breed Verification, and “Fit to Breed” clearance undergo automated or manual verification to prevent fraud and ensure authenticity.

3

AI-Powered Recommendations

Receive the top 3 most compatible matches for each pet using AI analysis of breed, age, size, location, and certification status. Interact through intuitive swipe-based Accept/Reject options, with mutual acceptance creating an official Match.

4

AI Assistant & Insights

Get advanced breed compatibility insights, trait predictions, and alerts for potential risks. The AI assists in evaluating pairing suitability to help users make informed and safe breeding decisions.

5

Digital Contracts

Every successful match generates a digital contract covering terms, responsibilities, deposits, and timelines. Contract statuses Draft, Signed, Active, Completed, or Cancelled ensure clear and secure agreement management.

6

Secure Escrow System

All payments and exchanges are handled through a protected escrow system. Whether it’s stud fees, puppy shares, or item-based compensation, assets are held securely and released only after confirmed breeding outcomes.

7

Health Safeguards

The platform enforces breeding cooldown periods, prevents overbreeding, and ensures pets are only eligible if their health certifications are valid. Pets undergoing a breeding cycle are automatically locked from matching until cleared.

8

Flexible Subscriptions

Choose from tiered access based on your needs: Free Tier : Basic features with limited slots and paid matching Standard (₱199/mo): Unlimited pet slots, free matching, and expanded details Premium (₱399/mo) :Unlimited matches, unlimited transactions, full analytics, and priority matching

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PAWLINK

Why did KHAT create PawLink?

+

We created PawLink after seeing many issues in the breeding community such as:

  • Inbreeding due to lack of information
  • Unverified breeders and unsafe practices
  • Fake certificates and identity fraud
  • Owners not knowing if their pets are fit to breed
  • Unfair transactions or broken agreements

PawLink exists to solve these problems and create a safe, transparent, and professional breeding ecosystem..

What does KHAT stand for?

+

KHAT represents the first letters of each team member’s name. This symbolizes teamwork, collaboration, and unity in building a platform that protects pets and supports responsible owners and breeders.

How is KHAT different from other pet platforms?

+

KHAT stands out by offering:

    • Full verification for users and pets
    • AI-powered match recommendations
    • Digital contracts for all breeding agreements
    • Escrow protection for payments
    • Health safeguards backed by vet-approved reminders
    • Support for owners, breeders, and shooters

No other local platform provides a complete end-to-end safe breeding system like PawLink.

How does the matching system work?

+

PawLink uses an AI-powered recommendation system that provides the top 3 best matches for each pet. It checks:

  • Breed
  • Age
  • Size
  • Location
  • Health verification
  • Genetic risk levels

If both users approve each other a Match is formed.

What happens when a match is confirmed?

+

PawLink automatically creates a digital contract that includes:

  • Breeding terms
  • Responsibilities
  • Deposit requirements
  • Start/end dates
  • Status updates (Draft → Signed → Active → Completed)

Both owners must sign it electronically.

How do transactions work?

+

PawLink supports:

  • Cash payments
  • Stud fees
  • Puppy-share agreements
  • Goods exchange (dog food, accessories)
  • Hybrid payments (ex: cash + puppy)

Every transaction goes through an escrow system, which safely holds payments until breeding is confirmed.

What is escrow and why is it important?

+

Escrow protects both parties. Funds, puppy agreements, or items are held securely and only released when:

  • Breeding is completed, and
  • Both parties confirm the outcome

How does PawLink protect pets from overbreeding?

+

The system enforces:

  • Cooldown periods
  • Breeding limits
  • Health checks

Only pets with valid health clearance can participate.
