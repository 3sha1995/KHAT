About KHAT

A research team committed to innovation and ethical technology

Who We Are

KHAT is a technology-driven team dedicated to improving pet safety and responsible breeding. We are a group of innovators, designers, and developers who saw the growing issues caused by unsafe, uninformed, and unregulated breeding practices. With compassion and modern technology at our core, we created KHAT to give pet owners, breeders, and shooters a safe and guided platform that protects animals and promotes ethical decisions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make pet breeding safe, transparent, and backed by science. We aim to reduce breeding risks, prevent unhealthy pairings, and promote ethical practices through verification, education, and intelligent digital tools. KHAT exists to ensure that every breeding decision is made responsibly‚Äîfor the wellbeing of pets and the people who care for them.

Our Approach

We combine advanced technology with a deep commitment to animal welfare. Our approach ensures accuracy, safety, and trust at every stage of the breeding process through:

Strict verification systems

All users, breeders, shooters, and pets undergo thorough ID and health verification to ensure authenticity and safety.

AI-Assisted Matching

Our solutions are built on extensive research, veterinary g uidance, and evidence-based practices to ensure reliability and effectiveness.

Ethical Standards

Transparency, accountability, and compassion guide every process, putting pet welfare above all.
